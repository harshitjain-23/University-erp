Certificate

Certification courses focus on specialized skills and knowledge, often requiring a shorter commitment than degrees. They enhance professional credibility and career advancement in specific industries or roles.

Diploma

Diploma programs provide practical training and skills in specific fields, often completed in one to two years. They are designed for quick entry into the workforce.

Degree

Degree programs offer comprehensive education and critical thinking skills over three to four years. They prepare graduates for advanced career opportunities and often lead to higher earning potential.
